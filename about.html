The restyle trait

We are a contemporary label based in Singapore exploring modularity in design. Taking a soft approach towards simplicity of life, TRT serves as a creative space channelling individuality and self-expression.

Our range of man and woman apparels are conceived in-house and designed with sustainability in mind. Curated edits of everyday separates, comfortable classics and functional outerwear encourage versatile styling, ensuring each piece is timeless and made to last.
